Meet Our Lawyers

Teamwork is at the core of our firm and has been for us, an effective marketing tool. A chamber that lacks the virtue of teamwork fails to gain or preserve the trust of its existing clients or potential.

Evans Anabah

Managing Partner

Mr. Evans Anabah was called to the Bar in the year 2009 and joined Kpatsa & Associates in 2010. Evans holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics and an LLB from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi, a Master of Applied Business Research from the Swiss Business School, Zurich, Switzerland, LLM in Oil and Gas Law from the Aberdeen business school of the Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen, Scotland. Evans also holds Certificates in litigation practise and Arbitration from the American University, Washington College of Law.

He is also an Engineering Procurement Construction contract specialist (EPC FIDIC SILVER BOOK). Evans is also an advocate and has been involved in many landmark cases. His area of interest include, corporate law, cross-border transactions, arbitration, EPC contracts and commercial law practice.

Evans, who has qualified in ADR and EPC contracts, represents banks and other private and public entities and sovereign governments in various financial transactions, with particular emphasis on their activities in the international capital market

Bettina Kwarteng

Associate

Bettina Kwarteng had her secondary education at St. Monica’s Secondary School, Mampong, Ashanti and has a Bachelor of Arts Degree (BA) from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), a Bachelor of Laws Degree (L.L.B) from the Faculty of Law, Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA) and was called to the Ghana Bar in October 2019.

Bettina also has a Master of Laws Degree (LL.M) in International Legal Studies from the American University – Washington College of Law in the United States of America, where she was awarded a full scholarship to attend. She has recently served as a Legal Intern to His Lordship Justice Samuel K. Marful-Sau, Justice of the Supreme Court of Ghana.

Her areas of interests include, Business and Corporate Law, Commercial Law, Immovable Property, Family Law, Asylum and Refugee Law, Human Rights Law and Arbitration.
